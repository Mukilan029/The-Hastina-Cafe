Chapter One

A rooftop built
on conviction.

"We didn't just open a restaurant. We opened a conversation — between the city and the sky, between a plate of food and a moment worth remembering."

Hastina Cafe was born from a simple belief: that vegetarian food deserves the same reverence, craft, and theatre as any culinary tradition. Perched on the fourth floor of the Udayam Complex in Chromepet, we built a rooftop where every dish tells a story.

Our menu draws from continental kitchens — hand-crafted burgers, silken pastas, cold-brew cocktails, and desserts that dissolve into memory. Every recipe is tested, rested, and tasted again. No shortcuts. No compromise on purity.

String lights trace the skyline above your table. The city hums below. The music lifts the evening. This is what we mean by honest food — made with pride, served with warmth, eaten with the sky above you.

100%
Pure Vegetarian — Always

Every ingredient, every stock, every sauce — certified vegetarian. No exceptions. Our kitchen has never touched meat.
